About Uku-Lins

Step into the groovy world of Uku-Lins, where the beachy vibes of the 1950s meet the sweet sounds of ukuleles and violins. Founded on the sunny shores, our store brings a splash of retro charm to string instruments. Whether you're dreaming of Hawaiian strums or classical melodies, we've got the gear to make your music wave.

Our passionate team is all about that laid-back lifestyle, offering expert advice with a side of nostalgia. Join us for a trip back in time with instruments that evoke sunsets and surf.

Ukuleles

Discover our collection of ukuleles, perfect for beach bonfires and backyard jams. From soprano to baritone, each uke captures the essence of tropical tunes with vibrant finishes and quality craftsmanship.

Whether you're a beginner picking up your first four strings or a pro seeking that perfect tone, our selection includes vintage-inspired models that transport you to the golden era of surf music.

Violins

Explore our range of violins, blending classical elegance with a retro twist. Handcrafted with care, these instruments deliver rich, resonant sounds ideal for orchestras or solo beachside serenades.

From student models to professional-grade fiddles, each violin is selected for its warmth and playability, evoking the spirited dances of yesteryear.

Repairs

Keep your instruments in top shape with our expert repair services. From string replacements to full restorations, our skilled luthiers handle it all with a retro flair.

We specialize in bringing back the shine to vintage ukuleles and violins, ensuring they play as good as new while preserving their classic character.
