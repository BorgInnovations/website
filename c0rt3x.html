About.

Synthesis consumes.

Synthesis - a complex whole formed by combining - is what our digital reality is becoming.

AIs are synthesizing "content" from human made works, with no regard for truth or accuracy.

This content has taken over our feeds and resources. Even the AIs themselves have become inundated by their own synthetic content.

Borg Innovations is taking a stand against such by developing a research index of analyzed non-AI generated resources for you, students, researchers, and anyone else who wants to break free from Synthesis.

So let's break free from Synthesis - Together.
