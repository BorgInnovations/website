About.

Synthesis consumes.

Synthesis - a complex whole formed by combining - is what our digital reality is becoming.

AIs are synthesizing "content" from human made works, with no regard for truth or accuracy.

This content has taken over our feeds and resources. Even the AIs themselves have become inundated by their own synthetic content.

Borg Innovations is taking a stand against such by developing a research index of analyzed non-AI generated resources for you, students, researchers, and anyone else who wants to break free from Synthesis.

So let's break free from Synthesis - Together.

AI Scoring.

NONE:

Content was produced prior to AI software use.

LOW:

Content was produced post AI software introduction.

Content was scanned for AI and triggers were minimal/non-existant.

MEDIUM:

Content was produced post AI software introduction.

Content was scanned for AI and several AI triggers were identified.

HIGH:

Content was produced prior to AI software use.

Content was scanned for AI usage and several major triggers were identified.

Content will be rejected from indexing.

Authenticity alert: An error in authenticity verification occured.

ConHowever, content does not appear to be AI generated.

Milestones.

Revised Search Content

11/1/25
Alpha 1.3 | Process 1.1

We have revised the search content to include title, author, and abstract. This will allow for more reliable queries.

We have revised the index crawling workflow. It is still clunky, but more reliable than it was. Expect further improvements on this. This will not impact users, this is a backend project.

Our AI bias now respects institutional scans. In other words, if an institution already implements AI scanning, we do not re-scan this material. This saves time and doesn't "reinvent the wheel."
