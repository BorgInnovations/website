A Digital Philosophy

Revisiting modern code standards

***************

Our modern code has become bloated and inefficient.

Videogames are a hundred gigabytes.

Websites have unnecessary features.

Idle tabs take up 600 megabytes of RAM.

We as programmers have forgotten the art of effeciant coding.

In this present time, developers merely tell users to install more RAM or get another hard-drive.

We are rediscovering the art of effecient code -

And this website is just the start.

The Philosophy.

Reduce

Reuse

Recycle

Essentially, code as though you have megabytes of storage and kilabytes of RAM.

This philosophy got astronauts to the moon in the 60s, surely it can take us to the stars.
